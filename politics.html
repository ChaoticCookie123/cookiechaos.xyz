Some definitions of political terms I use!

    Fascism
    A far right, capitalist, militaristic, nationalist dictatorship.
    Capitalism
    - A profit driven economy with private ownership of the means of production.
    Socialism
    - A non-profit driven economy with worker ownership of the means of production.
    Communism
    - An outcome driven economy with communal/social ownership of the means of production.
    - A stateless, classless, moneyless society.
    - A society that follows the principle of "from each according to their ability, to each according to their needs".
    Anarchy
    - A society lacking of hierarchy.
    Anarcho-Communism
    - A society lacking of hierarchy that follows the priniples of Communism.
    Left-Right Political Spectrum
    - The spectrum of hierarchy.
    - The more left a society is, the less hierarchy there is.
    - The more right a society is, the more hierarchy there is.
